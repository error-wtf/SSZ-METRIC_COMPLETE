# SSZ Neutron-Star Scale Domain

This domain represents relativistic compact stars (Neutron Stars) as a targeted scale use case within the static diagonal SSZ core framework.

## Scope & Purpose

- Classifies compact stellar surface boundaries into strong-field core, transition blend, or photon-sphere limits.
- Evaluates surface gravitational redshift predictions and surface dilation factors.

## Mathematical Formulation

- **Surface Redshift**:
  $$z_{\text{surface}} = \Xi(R)$$
- **Surface Dilation**:
  $$D(R) = \frac{1}{1 + \Xi(R)}$$

## Honest Limitations

- **No full nuclear equation of state model**: Surface structure is evaluated purely using geometric boundary coordinates.
- **No full rotating neutron star model**: Excludes non-spherical deformation or drag factors.
- **No full binary merger simulation**: Waveforms and dynamical mergers require N-body simulations.
- **No claim of complete observational proof**: This is a metric/regime/observable research framework.
